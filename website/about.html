About Fitsor

The Idea

After trying a handful of health and fitness applications over a few months I realized there was one gigantic flaw in logging exercise: they all required manually tracking your workouts every day.

There had to be a better way.

The Goal

The goal of Fitsor is to make manually tracking exercise a thing of the past, create a motivation ecosystem, and leverage data to define tangible plans backed by real results.

Wouldn't it be amazing to know exactly how many reps and time it'll take to reach your goal?
